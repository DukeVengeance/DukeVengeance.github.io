Making of Majuntang.com

In the making of the website you are probably surfing right now, we(I) encountered a lot of issues and obstructions.

Because Majuntang.com is designed with Dark Mode in mind, we find that after over twenty years, there's no standard way to style the scrollbar, if you see bright scrollbar in dark mode, it probably means your browser is incompatible with the methods Majuntang.com uses.

And, in the making of Card, auto-adjusting card with no media query, and scrollable only in the info description, we(I) made it via the flexbox, I don't know how I achieved that after I achieved that.

You can actually view Majuntang.com in Ancient Browsers™, but dark mode switch somehow disabled there in the middle of production. I cannot imagine people who are making websites targeting back to That Browser in Version 6™.

Majuntang.com uses Local Storage now for saving whether you are in dark mode or light mode, we(I) initially want to achieve this by cookies with expiry date set to browser process, but, since it feels so complicated with cookies than with local storage, you can localStorage.getItem(item_name) to get the particular value of the item_name, while with cookies the whole name value pairs will be given to you, which means you have to do the parsing yourself.

Anyway, I decide to put quite a lot of issues I encountered here with a catalogue, this page will probably update regularly.
